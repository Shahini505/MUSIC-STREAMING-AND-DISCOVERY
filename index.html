Frequently Asked Questions

How does the music discovery feature work?

Our music discovery feature uses algorithms based on your listening history and preferences to recommend new songs and artists that align with your taste.

Can I customize my music recommendations?

Yes, you can personalize your recommendations by liking or disliking songs, genres, and artists. The more you interact with the platform, the better it becomes at suggesting music you'll enjoy.

How often are new releases updated on the platform?

We strive to update our library regularly to include the latest releases. You can find new songs and albums as soon as they are available in the music scene.

What are featured playlists, and how are they curated?

Featured playlists are curated collections of songs based on themes, moods, and activities. Our team, as well as user contributions, curates these playlists to provide a diverse and engaging listening experience.

Can I create and share my playlists on the platform?

Absolutely! You can create your playlists and share them with the community. This feature allows you to showcase your favorite music and discover new tracks from others.

How do I find trending tracks on the platform?

Explore the "Trending Tracks" section to discover the hottest and most popular songs on our platform. This section is updated regularly based on user engagement and trends.

We pride ourselves on staying at the forefront of the music scene, constantly updating our library with the latest releases and uncovering hidden gems across genres. Our advanced algorithms understand your unique musical preferences, ensuring that every suggestion resonates with you on a personal level.
